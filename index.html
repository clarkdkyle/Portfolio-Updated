About Me

Not to long ago my basic bio would read "I am a nerd who normally spends most of his time reading comic books, playing video games, and listening to punk rock music." Now after having my first child I would like to say that I've changed as a person. But that would be a lie. Sure, I'm more responsible now that I have to be. But overall my interests and hobbies are still the same; I just don't have as much time to spend on them. This feels like enough filler text for this section.
